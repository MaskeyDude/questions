    Random Questions To Ask Your Friends When You're Bored

    These thoughtful questions will help you get even closer!

    JAMES ADAM •UPDATED: Apr 12, 2024

    Questions To Ask Friends

  • Other than yourself who has hurt you the most?
  • What would you do if you won a million dollars?
  • Who's your celebrity crush and what do you find most attractive in them physically and personality-wise?
  • What's something you hope will never change?
  • If someone offered you a chance to visit space, where would you go?
  • Do you believe in God?
  • What was your favorite toy as a kid?
  • Do you believe we would be better off if we didn't have social media?
  • If a turtle loses its shell, is it naked or homeless?
  • Do you have a favorite parent?
  • Which person helps you go through life even if they don't know?
  • Who was the last person that made you cry, and why?
  • What's the most recent dream you had?
  • 5 years from now if you were gifted a one-week trip to your dream place. Are you going solo or with someone, and who?
  • If you get to meet God, What would you say to him?
  • What parts of your life you would say are "perfect" just the way they are right now?
  • What are 2 of your biggest regrets?
  • Do you hang your toilet paper over or under?
  • What's your best memory so far of this year?
  • How many kids do you want?
  • If you had to pick three words to describe yourself, what would they be?
  • What's something you would do differently if you had the chance?
  • Do you think we will one day lose touch with each other? And why do you think that would be?
  • Have you ever had to look after an elderly or sick relative?
  • If you could be in someone else's shoes and know what they feel for a day, who would you pick and why?
  • What are you most grateful for?
  • Tell me about the dumbest thing you've ever done?
  • Which person is the closest to you?
  • Out of all the people you've lost, who do you miss the most? And what is it about them that you miss?
  • Is there anything that you've always wanted to ask me but haven't?
  • What is your favorite memory of me?
  • Is there something you want to say to me but are hiding?
  • What show are you currently binging?
  • Do you like who you are now?
  • Can you tell me about some people who have been particularly kind to you, and if they were here what would you say to them?
  • Which celebrity do you wish you could be friends with?
  • How has your life been different than what you'd imagined?
  • If you were to go back to the 1800s would you want to be a poet or someone's poetry?
  • Do you like to read? If so, what is your favorite book?
  • What advice would you give to your younger self?
  • What is one quality you like the most about yourself?
  • What is your happiest childhood memory?
  • Who has influenced you the most, and how?
  • Which apps or websites do you visit the most?
  • How religious are you from 1-10?
  • How was your first sexual experience?
  • What's your best dad joke?
  • What's something that always makes you smile?
  • How would you like to be remembered?
  • What is your deepest secret that is painful to share?
  • Is there a person you're glad isn't a part of your life anymore?
  • How many times do you check out people in real life or online?
  • Where would you go if you had to pack your stuff and relocate to another country tomorrow?
  • What is your favorite way to receive affection from your loved one?
  • What's one skill you would love to master?
  • Have you ever wished to die, and what was the reason for it?
  • What is your ideal romantic partner like?
  • What are some things that you truly want to achieve before dying?
  • What household chores do you hate the most?
  • In the last year what are some things you learned about yourself?
  • If your pet could talk, what would they say about you?
  • What is your biggest worry these days?
  • Do you think you deserve to live?
  • Who are your favorite relatives?
  • Coffee or tea?
  • Where did you grow up?
  • Is there a person you used to be close to but aren't right now, and why?
  • Do you think you give affection to your loved ones the way they deserve it?
  • How do you feel about people texting "K" to you?
  • Tell me something on your bucket list.
  • Pineapple on pizza... yay or nay?
  • Would you rather always smell like farts or a goat's mouth?
  • Is there a person you had intentionally put through pain? If so, Why?
  • Tell me about someone you still care about without saying their name?
  • Where do you think we will be in 10 years?
  • Is there someone who hurts you without them knowing?
  • Are you proud to have me in your life as your friend?
  • Can you tell me about the most difficult moments in your life?
  • If society didn't pressure you, Would you want to get married one day?
  • If you could talk to someone about anything you want and make them forget that conversation, who would you talk to?
  • What do you think love is?
  • What are some nicknames that you use for your friends or siblings?
  • Do you believe in love at first sight?
  • If this was to be our last conversation, is there anything you'd want to say to me?
  • Tell me about all the people you love the most even if they don't know?
  • Is your life going the way you wanted it to go?
  • If you could interview anyone from your life living or dead, but not a celebrity, who would it be and why?
  • What things do you want to have in your future partner?
  • If could say anything to your future partner, What would you say?
